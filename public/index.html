About Me

Hello, my name is Elias Dadoush and I'm 24 years old. I graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering from the American University of Madaba. After graduating I interned at Mobile Interactive Group where I worked as a technical assistant. My job was to edit, create, and update powerpoints and proposals. During my time there, I learnt how to work with others and manage my time. As I was searching for a job, I saw that software developers were in high demand, and I decided that it would be good if I improved my programming skills. I started off learning programming from youtube videos and udemy, then I got the opportunity to join a bootcamp course, which I would say I'm halfway through at the moment. I finished code101 and code201, and soon I'll start code301. Web development suits me well because I enjoy creating things that can be viewed and used by many people across the world. I started off hating css, but I've grown to like it and now enjoy designing websites
